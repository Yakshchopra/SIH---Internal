About Connekt

What is Connekt

In simple terms, Connekt is a web based application for colleges and the Directorate to keep a track of the Alumni.

Currently there are 7 Government colleges and 26 aided colleges under the Directorate of Higher Education, but there is no mechanism to keep track of them. As a solution, we have developed Connekt. Connekt allows the Alumni of these colleges to register themselves and also allows the Directorate to keep track of them. It also allows the students of these colleges to connect with the alumni.

To know more about us, feel free to "connekt" with us here.
